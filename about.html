HackerPack is the ultimate guide to hackathons, open-sourced and built on a community of hackathon hackers who want to learn and give back.

With HackerPack, you can:

Learn new technologies, regardless of prior experience

  • HackerPack aggregates everything by categories, enabling you to learn areas you have not heard about and better search for what you need.
  • Our recommendations are backed by data analysis on surveys filled out by other hackers, ensuring that the guide will give the best-curated advice.

Implement your dream ideas into reality

  • From surveys filled out by other hackers, we take projects considered most beginner-friendly and create a step-by-step guide for them.
  • We also select guides contributed from other hackers and give you diverse areas of projects to implement.

Contribute and establish your hacker reputation

  • We provide a platform where people can contribute their own guides and a feature for upvoting the most useful contributions, creating a community for hackathon hackers.
  • With Markdown implementation, you can easily format your guide and make it visually appealing.
