About Us

HackerPack is the ultimate guide to hackathons, open-sourced and built on a community of hackathon hackers who want to learn and give back. We want to be the go-to information database for all hackathon related tips, resources, and tutorials.

Our community platform will create a more inclusive hackathon environment for beginners. We envision HackerPack to be used to drive social change in the community by promoting hackathons to underrepresented students. Through HackerPack, we hope to increase diversity in hackathon, ultimately inspiring minority interest and confidence in computing degrees.

With HackerPack, you can:

Learn new technologies, regardless of prior experience

  • HackerPack aggregates everything by categories for easy search
  • We give the best-curated advice by giving recommendations backed by data on surveys filled out by other hackers

Implement your dream ideas into reality

  • Guides for beginner-friendly hackathon projects to get you from 0 to 1 all within the 24-36 hour timeframe of a hackathon
  • Made by hackers, for hackers.

Contribute and establish your hacker reputation

  • We provide a platform where people can contribute their own guides and a feature for upvoting the most useful contributions, creating a community for hackathon hackers.
  • With Markdown implementation, you can easily format your guide and make it visually appealing.
